LIVE PROJECTS

This live project was set by ISTD asking us to design a typographic work that explores the subject of ‘Taboos’. My taboo topic focused on 'menstrual cycle' and how in the 21st century it is forbidden or an uncomfortable subject to talk about. I created series of 3 campaign posters that eases this taboo subject combined with a hint of humor and other natural process that we all go through.
